AMALTHEA '16

SYMPOSIUM
Technologies in Defense and Internal Security


Amalthea ’16 hosts the second edition of Symposium, a step forward towards realizing its vision - ‘Spreading Knowledge’. Symposium provides a platform for research scholars and technology enthusiasts, with common interests and passions, to share and discuss their research. The audience is invited to present their research and find mentors in the speakers.

This edition of Symposium focuses on ‘Technologies in Defence and Internal Security’, a field where constant technological advancement is the need of the hour. In this Symposium, we seek to address the multifaceted issues and technologies that are associated with our country’s defence frontier, bringing together multiple disciplines and seeking out answers by marrying industry with academic pursuits.
