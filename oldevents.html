AMALTHEA '16

EVENTS


Alongside the Exhibition, Conclave and Symposium, Amalthea features events for people of various age groups to showcase the applicative knowledge acquired by them in various fields. Events like Robomilitia, Seismism, Quiz-o-Tech, InQuizzed, D'Code, etc. provide an opportunity to enthusiasts to reflect on their skills by competing and learning during the course of the events. The events keep getting better with passing years and are continuously improvised based on reviews from previous years. New events are also introduced to offer variety to the participants. With the aim of ‘Spreading Knowledge’- the motto of Amalthea, we promise a favourable platform for testing your skills this year again!

The full details of events with new and updated rules, incorporating changes based on feedback recieved last year, will be uploaded soon.
