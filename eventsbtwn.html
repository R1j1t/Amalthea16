InQuizzed
Team Quizzing Event
Event Details
InQuizzed @ Amalthea 2016

InQuizzed is one of the most popular events at Amalthea, witnessing enthusiastic participation from all over Gujarat each year! Get ready to tackle challenging questions that'll bamboozle and stupefy even the best out there. It'll surely be a great learning experience!

InQuizzed involves a written qualification round followed by multiple rounds on stage between the top contestants. Questions are based on General Knowledge, Technology and Logical Reasoning.

D'Code
Algorithmic Programming Contest
Event Details
D'Code @ Amalthea 2016

Are you the type of person who can code their way out of any problem- simple or complex? Then D'Code is the one thing to look out for! After a staggering nation-wide response last year, D'Code is back- and is going to be more challenging than ever before!

Quiz-O-Tech
Science & Technology Quiz for School Students
Event Details
Quiz-O-Tech @ Amalthea 2016

One of the most exciting events for school students, Quiz-O-Tech (Season 4) is back! Last year, a lot of innovative changes were introduced in Quiz-O-Tech- making the event much more competitive and interesting. With multiple rounds in different formats, multiple themes, innovative scoring schemes and audience participation, Quiz-O-Tech is a thriller for both the participants and the audience. Teams from schools all over Gujarat participate in this event each year and fight it out for the crown.

Topics range from Information Technology to Science and Research. Students from class 8 - 12 are eligible.

Detailed rules, eligibility criteria and sample questions will be uploaded soon. Watch this space for more information.

Seismism
Earthquake Resistant Modelling and Implementation Competition
Event Details
Seismism @ Amalthea 2016

Seismism was introduced as an experimental event in Amalthea 2015. It is back in Amalthea 2016 as a full event- and will be a lot bigger and much more professional this time around. Seismism is an event where participants can present their earthquake-resistant building ideas, make models, and test these for stability in earthquakes.

This time around, we will be introducing a professional shaking table and sophisticated materials as well as test parameters. Details about the improved Seismism (like materials, guidelines, test parameters, rules) will be uploaded soon. You don't need to be a Civil Engineer to participate- Seismism is a platform for learning and innovation for everyone.

Keep watching this space for updates.
